Understanding Your Health with AI

Welcome to the Health Report Assistant, your personal AI-powered tool designed to help you understand your medical reports and health queries better. This project leverages advanced artificial intelligence to provide accessible and relevant health information.

Simply upload your medical report in PDF format, and then ask any health-related questions you have. The chatbot will analyze the context from your report and, if needed, cross-reference it with general health guidelines from reliable sources like the World Health Organization (WHO) to give you comprehensive answers.

Your privacy and the security of your data are our top priority.

Please remember: This assistant provides information for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment.
